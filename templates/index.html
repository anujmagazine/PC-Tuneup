Apps That Start With Windows
The more apps that launch at startup, the longer your PC takes to be ready. You should disable ones you don't need.
How to disable a startup app:
  1. Click the button below to open Task Manager
  2. Click the "Startup" tab at the top
  3. Right-click any app you don't need at startup
  4. Click "Disable"
Keep enabled: Antivirus, audio drivers, cloud storage you use. Disable: Spotify, Discord, Adobe, Skype, Teams (if you don't need them immediately).
